TikPost – Content Automation Service

TikPost helps creators manage and streamline their posting workflow across multiple platforms. TikTok integration allows creators to securely authenticate their accounts and share content through approved API methods.

Features

• Secure login via TikTok Login Kit

• Upload videos as drafts (video.upload)

• Publish videos to the creator’s account (video.publish)
